About

SimpleCountdownTimer is a lightweight online countdown timer built for speed, clarity, and simplicity.

The timer runs directly in your browser — no downloads, no accounts, and no complicated settings. Whether you’re cooking, studying, working, or running an activity, the goal is the same: start a timer fast and get back to what you’re doing.

We aim to keep the experience clean and user-friendly, especially on mobile.

If you have feedback or feature requests, we’d love to hear from you.
