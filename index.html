FAQ

Does this timer run on my device?

Yes. The timer runs entirely in your browser. Your settings aren’t uploaded anywhere.

Will the timer keep running if my phone locks?

Some mobile browsers pause timers in the background. For best results, keep the page open and your device awake during critical timing.

Can I use it for Pomodoro?

Yes — try the 25m preset. You can also share preset links for Pomodoro rounds.
